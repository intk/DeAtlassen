Een ander gesicht van ’t Landts Magazyn_ vanuit het westen gezien

’s Lands Zeemagazijn, built in 1656, was designed by Daniel Stalpaert as a warehouse for the Admiralty of Amsterdam. 'This building is located on the River IJ-side of the island of Kattenburg, which was water until just a few years ago.' According to Olfert Dapper, so many carpenters and masons worked on the building that from the time the first stone was laid in 1655, 'it was put into use within nine months'. 'Behind this arsenal, on the waterside, the nation's warships are docked,' as many as seventy at a time.
