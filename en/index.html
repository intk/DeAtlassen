HSM Straet View

Here you can see the map of Amsterdam that the famous cartographer Joan Blaeu made in 1649.

We've added "Streete View" to this map so that you can also look at street scenes from Golden-Age Amsterdam. Drag the little figure to a marker on the map to see a print or etching of that location made by a seventeenth-century contemporary of Blaeu.

You can see more highlights from the works of famous cartographers like Mercator and Blaeu in the Atlases exhibit in Het Scheepvaartmuseum in Amsterdam.
