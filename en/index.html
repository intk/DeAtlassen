Het Scheepvaartmuseum Streete View

This is the map of Amsterdam made by the famous cartographer Joan Blaeu made in 1649.

We have added "Streete View" to this map so that you can have a look at street scenes from Golden-Age Amsterdam. Drag the little yellow figure to a marker on the map to see a print or etching of that location made by a 17th-century contemporary of Blaeu.

More highlights from the works of famous cartographers like Mercator and Blaeu are on view in the Atlases exhibition in Het Scheepvaartmuseum, the National Maritime Museum, in Amsterdam.
