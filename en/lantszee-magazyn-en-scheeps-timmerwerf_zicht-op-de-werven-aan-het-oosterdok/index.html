’s Lants Zee-magazyn en Scheeps Timmerwerf

On 12 August 1655, the Amsterdam city council gave the Admiralty of Amsterdam the entire western part of the island of Kattenburg so that it could build a carpenter's yard and the Zeemagazijn building. In exchange, the Admiralty gave the city council all the other land it possessed. 'To the north of this building are the Admiralty's ship carpentry yards and nearby are the mastmakers, smithies, and everything else which is necessary and useful for shipbuilding.' Ships in various stages of construction are in the yard.
