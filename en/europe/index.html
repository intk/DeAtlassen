Het Scheepvaartmuseum Streete View

This is the map of Europe made by the famous cartographer Blaeu in 1635.

Drag the little yellow figure to the marker on this map to see the map of Amsterdam that Joan Blaeu made in 1949.

We have added “Streete View” to this map so you can also have a look at street scenes from Golden-Age Amsterdam Drag the little figure to a marker on that map to see a print or etching of the location made by a 17th-century contemporary of Blaeu.

More highlights from the works of famous cartographers like Mercator and Blaeu are on view in the Atlases exhibition in Het Scheepvaartmuseum, the National Maritime Museum, in Amsterdam.
